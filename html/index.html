Investments

Tired of simply chopping wood like a total pleb? Well, why don't you try your hand at an investment and see how that goes. It can be a gamble most of the time, so try not to make into a habit.

Invest in a local dung farm.

A local drifter has recently bought some land but is short on gold to start his dung farm. If you decide to help him out with the missing gold, you have a chance to get yourself a nice return.
Chance of return: 50%
Return amount: 200

Invest

Invest in village tavern.

The tavern owner in the village has a habit of gambling. Thanks to this wonderful hobby he has inccured a large debt and is now desperate for some cash to pay off the people he has taken loans from. If you help him out with a small fee, he promises to pay you back double.
Chance of return: 50%
Return amount: 400

Price: 200

Help Tavern Owner

Throw money at the goblins outside of the village.

Goblins love money - a well known fact. Goblins also tend to hate owing people and return funds with interest.
As luck would have it, there is a goblin den outside of town, so why not go throw some gold at them. Hopefully, with their strong memory, and with a bit of luck they will come back to you with some interest. Hope they aren't the gambling kind...
Chance of profits: 50%
Return: Random Event

Price: 300

Throw Money at Goblins

Fund a mining expedition.

While walking down the village center, you notice a small booth selling mining expedition shares.
After speaking with one of the reprsentatives, he tells you that a recent soil and stone sample brought from the nearby mountains have supposedly yeilded in traces of a rare resource called "Via-Gra", usually only found in Morning Wood or in high mountain locations. The resourse is highly sought out in cities. The problem is that the mountain the samples are from tends to be rather dangerous and the resources required to actually start mining are missing.
They offer anyone from the local villages a chance to purcase a stake in the mine to raise the needed money for supplies and guards. In return, those who bought a stake will receive a portion of the overall gold.

Price: 400

Purchase a Stake

Guild Market

A market maintained by the National Merchants Guild. It offers a variety of services which could both make you some gold or simply help you advance in the world.

Buy shares in the local Quads market.

Buying quads from the local guild market can come in handy if you are looking to make a quick buck and or simply save the world from some being which can only be stopped with quads.
Quads can be stored and used for special events, or can be sold back to the guild for a random trade price giving you a chance to profit.

Buy Quads Sell Quads

Buy a Mystery Cart.

Mystery Carts - the guilds take on raffle. Mystery carts tend to come into existance when there is an large supply of certain resources in the guild stock. Myserty Carts came were made as a way to sell off these surplus supplies to make quick gold - Regular carts are filled with a variety of resources, which are later sold off to anyone willing to take a gamble with their gold.
Since everycart has something in it, you are at least guaranteed to make some of your money, if you don't end up making a profit.

Buy Mystery Cart

Gain access to Morning Wood.

By greasing a few palms with some cash you can gain access to the towns protected Morning Wood. Morning wood tends to be considered sacred, so it will earn you a nice +10 gold on the usual market price of wood. Just make sure you keep greasing those palms of you might end up losing access to it in the near future.

Buy a Cursed Artifact.

The continent has known many different civilizations throughout it's rich history. With a huge increase in grave robbers and relic hunters as of late, many ancient artifacts have made their way to the black market.
Each of these artifact on sale is said to have a "curse" placed on it.
The merchant assurses that some of there "curses" could bring about good fortune to the owner, so if you are up for gambling with your money and life, then why not purchase one for yourself. You could end up pleasantly surprised...or dead.

The Underground

WARNING: By completeing underground events you will earn notoriety, which in time, if it gets high enough, could cause you some serious problems with the law and the villagers!

Fight in the "Pitts of Blood".

The "Pitts of Blood" is the name of the local underground fighting circut hosted by corrupt officials for the all of the "good" folks which enjoy watching a bit of bloodshed amongst desprate contenders eager to earn some quick gold.
It's far from the most enjoyable place in the world, but it will help you get over a financial bump or two - if you survive that is.
